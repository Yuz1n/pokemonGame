What is the Daily Raffle?

The Daily Raffle is a reward for logging in once a day. Users that login more often get better rewards. The rewards are usually items and in-game coins.

How do I add/remove Friends?

You can go to their profile via Profile → Search Users → Enter the username. When the profile appears, Click on +Friend or -Friend whichever may apply.

You will also find a list of friends under Profile → Friends & Blocklist.
